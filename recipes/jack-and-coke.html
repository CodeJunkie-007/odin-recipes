Jack and Coke

Description

This is a recipe that combines Jack Daniels and Coke.

A few of these and you won't mind how basic this site looks!

Ingredients

  • Jack Daniels
  • Coke....the drink not the powder

Steps

  1. Mix the Jack Daniels and Code at a 1:2 ratio (example: 2 ounces of Jack with 4 ounces of Coke)
  2. Drink
  3. Repeat as needed
